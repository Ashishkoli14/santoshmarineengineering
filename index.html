About Us

Crafting India’s Maritime Strength with Power, Precision & Trust

Santosh Marine and Engineering Consultants Private Limited is a full-spectrum maritime consulting firm headquartered in Mumbai, India. We support shipowners, shipyards, operators, investors, ports, and maritime service providers with expert advisory across every stage of the maritime value chain.

We combine deep industry expertise with practical execution capabilities to deliver technically sound, commercially viable, and regulatory-compliant solutions. Our independent, conflict-free approach ensures your interests are always at the forefront.

Focused on increasing shipyard efficiency, boosting shipbuilding output, and driving cost reduction through smart engineering and operational excellence — built to endure, built to lead.
